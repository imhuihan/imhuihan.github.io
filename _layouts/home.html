Hello! I am a Ph.D. candidate in Economics at Boston University. My research interests are in industrial organization and household finance. A Retrospective Analysis of U.S. Bank Mergers (with Tim Lee) I make use of micro-data and employ structural techniques to quantitatively study the welfare implications of policies in the financial markets. In my current research, I use novel models to study price dispersion and information asymmetry in the US mortgage market.

I hold both MSc and BSc in Econometrics and Mathematical Economics from the London School of Economics and Political Science .
